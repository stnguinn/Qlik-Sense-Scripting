Qlik Sense Steps
To incorporate a .PNG image into Qlik Sense, follow these steps:

   1. Prepare the Image:
    Ensure that the .PNG image is saved in a location accessible to Qlik Sense.

    2.Open Qlik Sense Desktop or Qlik Sense Hub:
    Launch Qlik Sense Desktop or access the Qlik Sense Hub, depending on your workflow.

    3. Create a New Sheet or Open an Existing One:
    Open the Qlik Sense app where you want to add the image. Create a new sheet or navigate to an existing sheet.

    4, Add a Text & Image Object:
        Drag and drop a "Text & Image" object from the assets panel onto the sheet.

    5. Configure the Text & Image Object:
        Double-click on the added "Text & Image" object to open the properties panel.
        In the properties panel, go to the "Image" tab.

    6. Link to the Image:
        Under the "Image" tab, find the "Link" section.
        Input the URL or local path to the .PNG image in the "Link" field.

    7. Adjust Size and Position (Optional):
        You can adjust the size and position of the image within the object using the options available in the properties panel.

    8. Apply Changes:
        Click "Apply" or "OK" to save the changes to the properties.

    9. Preview and Save:
        Preview the sheet to ensure that the image is displayed correctly.
        Save the Qlik Sense app to retain the changes.
